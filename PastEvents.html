Kalyan

Kalyan is an event which held an interschool competition for different events in which students from various schools in Rourkela had participated. It was heart warming to see even the differently abled students taking interest and taking part in the event. This show the dedication and hardwork of the students and that they nothing can stop them from atleast trying their best. The event also included drawing and singing competitions from the children. Various organizations and clubs had been invited to the event and donations were also made to different schools. Proper lunch were served to all the delicate guests and the students who had shown interest to take part in the event and to everyone who had contributed to make the event a success.
